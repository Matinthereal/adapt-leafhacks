01

The nine beats

The whole film on one spine. Each beat is timecoded with its three lanes — what's on screen, the exact voiceover, and the on-screen text. Amber nodes are the human/heart beats; cyan is the hero moment.

02

The voiceover, clean

One flowing read — a paragraph per beat. Short sentences with natural stresses; a nervous-but-proud 17-year-old will nail it. Record in ~9 segments and stitch, no single perfect take needed.

03

The locked demo

Type these two lines to Gem — and never say chlorophyll. That's the guaranteed on-stage reveal: the gap report surfaces exactly the thing you left out.

Turn 2 — the fuzzy bit (on purpose)
